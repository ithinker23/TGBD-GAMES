About TGBD Games

Golden Blade Devs aspire to become Game creators and develop unique and quality game concepts

Established In 2018

Our company has been established or mentally established on mid 2018, BC Canada. Founded by 2 High School Students with aspiring ideas and goals of creating and playing Games.

Our Goal

Our Goal is not to just make Games, but teach and inspire other people too to learn code and make video games. We're focused not just on creating Game-Based Applications but other programs and technologies as well as helping other people to learn and play.
