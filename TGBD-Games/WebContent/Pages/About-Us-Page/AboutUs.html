Why We Do What We Do

About.

Golden Blade Devs is a group of developers who share the same  interest of creating video games. Golden Blade Devs was founded by a pair of Indie Devs that goes by the name Vorezii (Teevint Prak) and IThinker23 (Ishan Rana). Our games at times are open-source games allowing for other developers to play around. We also have game assets and game template for everyone to access.

Established in 2018

The group has been established or mentally established on mid 2018, BC Canada. Founded by 2 High School Students with aspiring ideas and goals of creating and playing Games. 

Our Goal

Our Goal is not to just make Games, but teach and inspire other people too to learn code and make video games. We're focused not just on creating Game-Based Applications but other programs and technologies as well as helping other people to learn and play.
